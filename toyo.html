The History of Toyota: A Legacy in Motion

1930s – The Beginning: Toyota began as Toyoda Automatic Loom Works. Kiichiro Toyoda launched the automotive division in 1937 with the first car, the AA.

1950s-1970s – Global Rise: Entered the U.S. with the Crown (1958) and introduced the Corolla (1966), establishing Toyota's reputation for reliability.

1980s-2000s – The 3rd Age: This era saw the Supra, Camry, and RAV4 rise. The three-oval logo debuted in 1989, symbolizing customer, company, and world unity.

Today (2025): A minimalist black logo reflects modernity, with Toyota leading in hybrid technology and sustainability.
